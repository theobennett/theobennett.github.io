Our Expertise

What We Treat

We provide adult psychiatric care across a full range of conditions, with particular depth in treatment-resistant and complex presentations.

Many of our patients have seen other providers before finding us. Whether you're newly seeking care or looking for a different approach after other treatments haven't worked, we're equipped to help.

Depression

Major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder (dysthymia), postpartum depression, and seasonal affective disorder.

Treatment-Resistant Depression

For patients who have tried two or more adequate antidepressant trials without sufficient response. We specialize in this complex presentation, with access to TMS and emerging interventional options.

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), panic disorder, social anxiety disorder, health anxiety, and specific phobias.

ADHD

Adult ADHD — including late-diagnosed adults who are encountering it for the first time. Comprehensive evaluation, diagnosis, and ongoing medication management.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar I, Bipolar II, and cyclothymia. Careful mood stabilization with attention to metabolic effects and quality of life.

PTSD & Trauma

Post-traumatic stress disorder, complex PTSD, and trauma-related mood and anxiety disorders. Pharmacotherapy and trauma-informed psychotherapy.

OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder — medication management and therapy, including coordination with ERP-trained therapists.

Insomnia & Sleep Disorders

Chronic insomnia, sleep disruption related to mood and anxiety disorders, and hypersomnia. CBT-I and pharmacotherapy.

Substance Use Disorders

Co-occurring substance use and psychiatric conditions. Medication-assisted treatment and integrated care coordination.

Schizophrenia & Psychosis

Schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorder, and other psychotic disorders. Comprehensive medication management and coordination with community supports.

Eating Disorders

Psychiatric co-management of anorexia, bulimia, and binge eating disorder. We coordinate closely with specialized eating disorder therapists and dietitians.

Burnout & Life Transitions

Occupational burnout, grief, major life transitions, and adjustment disorders. Not every presentation requires a diagnostic label — we treat people, not just diagnoses.

Clinical Focus

Treatment-Resistant Depression

If you've tried multiple medications without adequate relief, you're not alone — and you're not out of options. TRD is a specialty of ours. We approach it systematically: re-evaluating diagnosis, reviewing prior treatment history, considering pharmacogenomic testing, and planning a structured path forward — including interventional options (TMS, esketamine) as they become available.
