What We Specialize In

We welcome referrals for patients with:

  • Treatment-resistant depression (2+ failed med trials)
  • Complex mood and anxiety disorders
  • ADHD diagnosis and medication management
  • Bipolar disorder (all subtypes)
  • PTSD and trauma-related conditions
  • OCD (medication management)
  • Personality disorders (with co-occurring mood/anxiety)
  • Medication optimization and management

Note: We provide medication management and psychiatric evaluation. For ongoing psychotherapy, we coordinate with your therapists or provide referrals as needed.

How to Refer a Patient

Option 1: Secure Electronic Referral (Preferred)

Use our secure referral portal: Coming soon (please email us in the meantime)

You'll provide the patient's basic information, your clinical concern, and any relevant history. The patient receives a secure link to complete intake. We review and call both you and the patient within 24 hours.

Option 2: Email Referral

Send a brief email to: [your-practice-email@altos.com]

Include:

  • Patient name and date of birth
  • Contact information (phone/email)
  • Brief reason for referral ("TRD, 2 failed trials" or "ADHD evaluation")
  • Any recent relevant information (current meds, previous psych history)
  • Your name and contact info

Option 3: Fax or Phone

If email isn't your style:

  • Fax: [Your fax number]
  • Phone: [Your phone number]

What Happens Next

  1. We receive and review your referral (usually same day)
  2. We contact the patient within 24 hours to schedule an appointment
  3. Patient is seen within 1–2 weeks (urgent cases sooner)
  4. You receive a summary of our initial evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment plan
  5. We stay in touch as treatment progresses. Call or email if you have questions anytime.

Insurance & Payment (For Providers)

We're a private-pay practice. Your patients will pay out-of-pocket for our services, and we provide superbills for insurance reimbursement.

Good news for your patients: Many have out-of-network mental health benefits that reimburse 50–80% of the cost. We help them understand their benefits and maximize reimbursement.

Your role: You don't need to worry about insurance coordination. We handle that directly with the patient. You just focus on collaborative care.
