Getting reimbursed by your insurance

We provide superbills — detailed receipts with CPT and diagnosis codes — that you can submit to your PPO insurance for out-of-network reimbursement. To access yours:

  1. Log in to the Patient Portal above
  2. Navigate to Billing → Documents
  3. Download your superbill as a PDF
  4. Submit via your insurance carrier's member portal or mail

Questions? Send us a secure message through the portal or call our office at (650) 555-0000.
