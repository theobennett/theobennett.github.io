For Existing Patients

Access your patient portal through SimplePractice. Log in to schedule appointments, message your provider, request medication refills, and view your health records.

Access Your Portal

First time logging in? You'll receive an email invitation from SimplePractice. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact our staff.

What You Can Do in Your Portal

📅 Schedule & Manage Appointments

View your upcoming appointments, cancel or reschedule with ease. See available times and book directly if there are openings.

💬 Secure Messaging

Send messages to your provider between appointments. Get responses usually within 24 hours. It's like having email with your psychiatrist.

💊 Medication Refills

Request refills directly through the portal. We'll review and send to your pharmacy. No more phone calls.

📋 View Your Records

Access your treatment notes, assessment results, and care summaries anytime. All your information in one place.

📝 Complete Forms

Fill out intake forms, symptom check-ins, and surveys directly in the portal. Save time at your appointment.

🔒 Secure & Private

All communication is HIPAA-encrypted. Your privacy and security are our top priority.

Need Help with the Portal?

Common Issues

I haven't received my login invitation

Check your spam folder first. If it's not there, contact our staff at [your-email@altos.com] and we'll resend it.

I forgot my password

On the SimplePractice login page, click "Forgot your password?" and follow the instructions to reset it. If you need help, contact us.

I can't find my appointment history

Log in, go to "Appointments," and select "Past Appointments" to view your history.

I have a technical issue

Try refreshing your browser or logging out and back in. If the problem persists, contact SimplePractice support directly (link available in your portal) or reach out to our staff.

Questions? Email us at [your-email@altos.com] or call us. Our staff is here to help.
