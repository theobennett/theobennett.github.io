Our Philosophy

Our Care Models

A foundation of integrated, personalized, and data-driven psychiatric care.

Most psychiatric practices treat one dimension of a person at a time. We designed our care model to do the opposite — to hold the full picture, coordinate across domains, and adapt as you change.

Framework

Three pillars of care

Every clinical decision we make is shaped by these interconnected principles.

Integrated

Medication, therapy, and interventional treatments coordinated within a single practice — not siloed across disconnected providers.

Data-Driven

Pharmacogenomics, outcome tracking, and objective measurement replace guesswork with precision at every stage of treatment.

Collaborative

You are an active partner in every decision. We educate, explain, and build treatment plans together — never unilaterally.

How it works in practice

Each pillar translates into concrete clinical processes that shape your experience from the first visit forward.

We believe mental wellness is intrinsically linked to overall health. Our integrated model ensures that your psychiatric care is seamlessly coordinated with other aspects of your well-being.

We collaborate with primary care physicians, therapists, and specialists to create a holistic view of your health, ensuring that all treatment decisions are made with a comprehensive understanding of you as an individual.

  • Medication, therapy, and interventional services under one roof
  • Metabolic health, sleep, nutrition, and lifestyle treated as clinical variables
  • Coordinated communication with your PCP and other specialists
  • Unified treatment record — no information gaps between providers

Honed through clinical practice at Stanford and other world-class institutions, our approach leverages data to move beyond a one-size-fits-all model.

Where appropriate, we utilize tools like genetic testing (pharmacogenomics) to predict medication response, quantitative measures to track symptom change, and structured outcome tracking to objectively measure progress. This commitment to data allows for a more precise, predictable, and effective treatment journey.

  • Pharmacogenomic testing to predict medication response
  • Standardized symptom scales administered at regular intervals
  • Quantitative outcome tracking — not just subjective impressions
  • Treatment adjustments driven by measurable trends, not intuition alone

At Altos Psychiatry, you are an active partner in your care. We prioritize patient education, ensuring you understand the "why" behind every aspect of your treatment plan.

Our model is built on a foundation of trust, transparency, and shared decision-making, empowering you with the knowledge and confidence to take control of your mental health.

  • Clear explanations of diagnosis, medication rationale, and expected outcomes
  • Shared decision-making at every treatment juncture
  • Responsive access between visits via the patient portal
  • Ongoing psychoeducation — we teach, not just treat

Psychiatric symptoms don't exist in isolation. Sleep disruption, metabolic changes, hormonal shifts, substance use, and life stressors all shape how conditions present and respond to treatment.

We factor these variables into every clinical decision — not as wellness add-ons, but as core components of evidence-based psychiatry.

  • Sleep architecture assessed and addressed early
  • Nutritional and exercise patterns reviewed as clinical data
  • Occupational and relational context considered in treatment planning
  • Referrals to specialists (endocrinology, sleep medicine) when warranted

What this looks like for you

First Visit

A 60–90 minute comprehensive evaluation. We review your full history, discuss goals, and build an initial treatment framework — together.

Ongoing Care

Regular follow-ups calibrated to your needs. Outcome measures tracked visit-to-visit. Treatment adjusted based on data, not guesswork.

Between Visits

Secure messaging through our patient portal. Responsive communication for medication questions, side effects, or emerging concerns.

Coordination

With your permission, we communicate directly with your therapist, PCP, or other specialists to ensure aligned, efficient care.
