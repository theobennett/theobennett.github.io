In Practice

How each pillar shapes your care

Concrete clinical processes — from the first visit forward.

Mental wellness is intrinsically linked to overall health. Our integrated model coordinates psychiatric care with every aspect of your well-being — not as an afterthought, but as foundational architecture.

  • Medication, therapy, and interventional services under one roof
  • Sleep, nutrition, and lifestyle treated as clinical variables
  • Direct coordination with your PCP and specialists
  • Unified treatment record — no information gaps between providers

Shaped by clinical training at Stanford and leading academic institutions, our approach uses objective data to move beyond one-size-fits-all psychiatry. Genetic testing predicts medication response, standardized scales quantify change, and structured tracking replaces guesswork with precision.

  • Pharmacogenomic testing to guide medication selection
  • Validated symptom scales at regular intervals
  • Quantitative outcome tracking — not subjective impressions alone
  • Adjustments driven by measurable trends

You understand the rationale behind every recommendation. We prioritize patient education and shared decision-making — empowering you with the knowledge and confidence to take an active role in your care.

  • Clear explanations of diagnosis, rationale, and expected outcomes
  • Shared decision-making at every treatment juncture
  • Responsive between-visit access via the patient portal
  • Ongoing psychoeducation — we teach, not just treat

Psychiatric symptoms don't exist in isolation. Sleep disruption, metabolic shifts, hormonal changes, and life stressors all shape how conditions present and respond to treatment. We factor these variables into every clinical decision — as core evidence-based psychiatry, not wellness add-ons.

  • Sleep architecture assessed and addressed early
  • Nutrition and exercise patterns reviewed as clinical data
  • Occupational and relational context in treatment planning
  • Specialist referrals when warranted
