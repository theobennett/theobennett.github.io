Our Care Models

A foundation of integrated, personalized, and data-driven psychiatric care.

The Integrated Care Model

We believe mental wellness is intrinsically linked to overall health. Our integrated model ensures that your psychiatric care is seamlessly coordinated with other aspects of your well-being. We collaborate with primary care physicians, therapists, and specialists to create a holistic view of your health, ensuring that all treatment decisions are made with a comprehensive understanding of you as an individual.

Data-Driven Precision

Honed through clinical practice at Stanford and other world-class institutions, our approach leverages data to move beyond a one-size-fits-all model. Where appropriate, we utilize tools like genetic testing (pharmacogenomics) to predict medication response, quantitative EEG (qEEG) to identify neurological biomarkers, and outcomes tracking to objectively measure progress. This commitment to data allows for a more precise, predictable, and effective treatment journey.

The Collaborative Partnership

At Altos Psychiatry, you are an active partner in your care. We prioritize patient education, ensuring you understand the "why" behind every aspect of your treatment plan. Our model is built on a foundation of trust, transparency, and shared decision-making, empowering you with the knowledge and confidence to take control of your mental health.
