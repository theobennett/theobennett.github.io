Our Care

Psychotherapy

Evidence-based individual and group therapy — often most powerful when integrated with medication care.

Individual therapy

Our individual therapy draws primarily from psychodynamic and cognitive-behavioral frameworks. Psychodynamic therapy explores the patterns, relationships, and experiences that shape how you think, feel, and respond to the world. CBT provides concrete tools to recognize and shift unhelpful patterns of thought and behavior. Most of our work integrates elements of both.

Therapy at Altos is active and goal-oriented. We don't aim for indefinite open-ended exploration — we work toward clear, meaningful change, with checkpoints to assess whether we're getting there.

Integrated care

Many patients benefit most from medication and therapy together — and the two work best when they're coordinated by the same practice. When your prescriber and therapist share clinical context and a common framework, treatment decisions are better informed and progress tends to be faster.

If you already have a therapist you trust, we're also well set up to work alongside them as your medication management partner.

Group therapy

Group therapy programs are in development for specific conditions including depression, anxiety, and trauma. Groups offer the benefits of shared experience, normalization, and peer support — alongside skilled clinical facilitation. Details on group offerings will be posted as programs launch.
