Medication Management

What is Medication Management?

Think of it like having a personal health coach for your mind's chemistry. Medication management is a partnership between you and your psychiatrist to find the right medication, at the right dose, to help you feel your best. It’s not just about writing a prescription; it's an ongoing process of monitoring, adjusting, and ensuring your treatment plan is working for you.

Who is it for?

This service is for anyone dealing with conditions like depression, anxiety, ADHD, bipolar disorder, or other mood disorders where medication can be a helpful part of the treatment. It's especially important for those who have tried medications before without success or who are sensitive to side effects.

How does it work?

We start with a detailed conversation about your symptoms, history, and goals. Based on this, we may recommend a medication. We'll have regular check-in appointments to see how you're feeling, discuss any side effects, and make small adjustments as needed until we find the perfect balance.
