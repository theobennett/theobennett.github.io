Every service, one team

Rather than referring you across multiple providers, we coordinate medication, therapy, and interventional treatments within a single practice — so your whole picture is visible to the people making clinical decisions about your care.

Core Service

Medication Management

Precision psychopharmacology informed by your full clinical picture. We don't just prescribe — we monitor, adjust, and manage side effects systematically. Where appropriate, we use pharmacogenomic testing to predict medication response and reduce trial-and-error.

Core Service

Psychotherapy

Individual and group therapy grounded in psychodynamic and cognitive-behavioral frameworks. We offer therapy as a standalone service or integrated with medication management. Group therapy programs for specific conditions are in development.

Learn more
Coming 2026

TMS Therapy

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive treatment for major depression and OCD. We will offer standard TMS and the accelerated SAINT protocol (Stanford Accelerated Intelligent Neuromodulation Therapy) beginning in 2026.

Learn more
Future

Psychedelic-Assisted Therapy

We are tracking the evolving regulatory landscape for psychedelic-assisted therapy in California, including psilocybin and ketamine-assisted treatment. We intend to offer these services as legal frameworks mature and evidence continues to build.
