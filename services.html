Core Service

Medication Management

Precision psychopharmacology informed by your full clinical picture. We don't just prescribe — we monitor, adjust, and manage side effects systematically. Where appropriate, we use pharmacogenomic testing to predict medication response and reduce trial-and-error.

  • Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation
  • Ongoing medication optimization
  • Pharmacogenomic testing when indicated
  • Coordination with your therapist and PCP

Built Into Every Visit

Tools that sharpen your care

These aren't separate services — they're woven into how we practice, available to every patient when clinically appropriate.

Outcome Tracking

Validated instruments — PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5, and others — measured at every visit. Your progress is visible in data, not just subjective impressions.

Pharmacogenomics

Genetic testing that predicts how your body metabolizes psychiatric medications — reducing trial-and-error and guiding more precise prescribing decisions.

Telehealth

Secure video visits for medication management and therapy from anywhere in California. Same quality of care, same provider continuity, more flexibility.

On the Horizon

Advanced modalities

We are building the clinical infrastructure to offer these treatments as they become available — responsibly, and grounded in evidence.

Launching 2026

TMS Therapy

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive treatment for major depression and OCD. We will offer both standard TMS and the accelerated SAINT protocol — the Stanford-developed approach delivering a full treatment course in five days.

  • FDA-cleared for depression and OCD
  • Non-invasive — no anesthesia or sedation
  • Sessions under 20 minutes
  • Standard and accelerated (SAINT) protocols
Learn About TMS
Future

Psychedelic-Assisted Therapy

Emerging evidence supports the use of ketamine, psilocybin, and MDMA-assisted therapy for treatment-resistant conditions. We are tracking the evolving regulatory landscape in California and intend to offer these services as legal frameworks mature.

  • Evidence-first — not hype-driven

Our Approach

Integrative by design

Every service is delivered within our integrated care model — factoring in metabolic health, sleep, nutrition, and lifestyle as core clinical variables, not peripheral add-ons. Your treatment plan is coordinated across modalities by providers who share the same record and talk to each other.

This is how psychiatric care was meant to work. We just built a practice that makes it possible.
