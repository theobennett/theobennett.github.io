Our Services

A curated range of evidence-based treatments, delivered within an integrated and personalized care model.

Every service, one team

Rather than referring you across multiple providers, we coordinate medication, therapy, and interventional treatments within a single practice. This means your whole picture is visible to the people making clinical decisions about your care.

Core Service

Medication Management

Precision psychopharmacology informed by your full clinical picture. We don't just prescribe — we monitor, adjust, and manage side effects systematically. Where appropriate, we use pharmacogenomic testing to predict medication response and reduce trial-and-error.

TMS Therapy 2026

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive treatment for major depression and OCD. We will offer standard TMS and the accelerated SAINT protocol (Stanford Accelerated Intelligent Neuromodulation Therapy) beginning in 2026.
