Dr. Anya Sharma, MD

Founder & Psychiatrist

Dr. Sharma founded Alto Psychiatry with the vision of creating a mental health practice that is both deeply compassionate and scientifically rigorous. She completed her medical training at Stanford University and her residency in psychiatry at UCSF, where she specialized in mood disorders and innovative treatments.

Her philosophy is centered on partnership. She believes that the best treatment plans are built collaboratively with her patients, combining evidence-based medication management with supportive therapy to treat the whole person, not just the symptoms. She has a special interest in treatment-resistant depression and the potential of new therapies like TMS.
