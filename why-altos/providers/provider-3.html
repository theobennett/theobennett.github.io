Dr. Maria Flores, PhD

Lead Therapist & TMS Specialist

Dr. Maria Flores is a licensed clinical psychologist who leads the psychotherapy and TMS programs at Alto Psychiatry. She earned her PhD in Clinical Psychology from UCLA, with a focus on cognitive-behavioral and acceptance-based therapies for anxiety and depression.

Dr. Flores is dedicated to providing therapy that is active, collaborative, and goal-oriented. She helps clients build concrete skills to manage difficult emotions and change unhelpful patterns of thinking. As the head of the TMS program, she guides patients through the process with expertise and care, ensuring they feel informed and supported at every step.
