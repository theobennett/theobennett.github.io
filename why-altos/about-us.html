Our Story

A practice built around the care most psychiatrists want to give — and most patients actually need.

Our Mission

Psychiatry that meets you where you are

Most psychiatric practices are built around the schedule, not the patient. Appointments every 4–6 weeks. Voicemail during a crisis. A provider who sees so many patients they can't remember the nuance of your situation.

We started Altos Psychiatry because we believed it was possible to do better — to run a financially sustainable practice and actually be there for patients when it matters.

Core Values

What drives our care

Availability

Mental health crises don't respect business hours. We build systems that let us be reachable for the moments that matter, without burning out our providers.

Whole-Person Care

Sleep, nutrition, metabolic health, and exercise are not peripheral to psychiatry — they are core variables. We treat them as such.

Continuity

Your psychiatric history is yours. If you move, need a hand-off, or seek a second opinion, we facilitate seamless transitions — not blank slates.

Clinical Rigor

Innovation without evidence is not care. We adopt new treatments when the data supports them, and we track outcomes to know whether they're working for you.

Where We Practice

Menlo Park · Silicon Valley

We are based in Menlo Park, California, serving adults throughout the Peninsula and Silicon Valley. Our patient population skews toward high-functioning professionals navigating the particular pressures of high-performance careers — the gap between how capable they appear and how they actually feel.

We understand that context. We've also built the practice to serve patients across California via telehealth.
