Who we look for — and who we are

Clinical Rigor

Every provider at Altos trained at respected academic medical centers and holds board certification in their specialty. We stay current with the evidence and are willing to say when we don't know.

Genuine Availability

We cap panel sizes deliberately. A provider who is spread too thin cannot give patients the attention they deserve — and will burn out. We treat provider wellbeing as a clinical quality issue.

Long-Term Thinking

Our goal is not to manage your condition indefinitely. It is to help you achieve durable remission — and to equip you with the understanding of your own neurobiology to sustain it.
