For Referring Providers

Partnering in Patient Care

At Altos Psychiatry, we believe in a collaborative approach to healthcare. We value our relationships with primary care physicians, therapists, and other specialists in the community. Our goal is to serve as a trusted partner, providing your patients with expert psychiatric care while maintaining open lines of communication.

Our Areas of Expertise

We welcome referrals for patients requiring specialized care in:

  • Treatment-Resistant Depression (including TMS and psychedelic-assisted therapy evaluations)
  • Complex Mood and Anxiety Disorders
  • ADHD Diagnosis and Management
  • Comprehensive Medication Management
  • Integrated Psychiatric and Therapeutic Care
