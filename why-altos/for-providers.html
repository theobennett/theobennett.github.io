For Referring Providers

We respond to referrals within one business day and keep you in the loop on your patient's care — with their consent.

What We Treat Well

High-priority referrals

  • → Treatment-resistant depression (2+ failed trials)
  • → Complex or refractory anxiety disorders
  • → Medication-related metabolic side effects
  • → Diagnostic clarification (mood / anxiety / ADHD)
  • → PTSD in high-functioning adults
spacer

General referrals

  • ✓ Adult ADHD — diagnosis and management
  • ✓ Bipolar spectrum disorders
  • ✓ Integrated med + therapy care
  • ✓ Patients needing concierge-level access
  • ✓ General adult outpatient psychiatry

Payment note: Altos Psychiatry is a private-pay practice. Patients pay at time of service and receive superbills for PPO out-of-network reimbursement. We do not participate in Medi-Cal or HMO panels. For patients where cost is a barrier, please reach out and we'll discuss.
