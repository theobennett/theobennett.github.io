Why Partner With Us

A psychiatric practice that actually communicates

We know how frustrating it can be to refer a patient to psychiatry and never hear back — or to receive updates that don't meaningfully inform your own care decisions. We're built differently.

With patient consent, we provide referring providers with a treatment summary after the initial evaluation and communicate significant care plan changes. We return your calls. We close the loop.
