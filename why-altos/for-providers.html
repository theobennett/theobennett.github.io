Clinician Partnerships

For Referring Providers

We partner closely with primary care physicians, therapists, and specialists across the Peninsula to provide coordinated, high-quality psychiatric care.

A trusted partner in your patient's care

We understand the challenge of finding a psychiatric practice that is genuinely responsive to referring providers. At Altos Psychiatry, we take co-management seriously: timely intake, clear communication, and closed-loop updates when clinically appropriate and consented by the patient.

Clinical Focus

What we treat well

High-priority referrals

  • → Treatment-resistant depression (2+ failed trials)
  • → Complex or refractory anxiety disorders
  • → Medication-related metabolic concerns
  • → Diagnostic clarification (mood vs. anxiety vs. ADHD)

General referrals

  • ✓ ADHD — adult diagnosis and management
  • ✓ Bipolar spectrum disorders
  • ✓ PTSD and trauma-related disorders
  • ✓ Patients needing integrated med + therapy
How to Refer

Straightforward process

1

Contact us directly

Call our office at (650) 555-0000 or email referrals@altospsychiatry.com. A brief clinical summary is helpful but not required at this stage.

2

We confirm fit and availability

We'll confirm within one business day whether we can see the patient and provide an expected intake timeline. For urgent cases, we'll flag that directly.

3

Coordinated, closed-loop care

With the patient's consent, we'll send you a treatment summary after the initial evaluation and communicate significant changes in the care plan. We welcome your ongoing clinical input.

Payment note for referring providers: Altos Psychiatry is a private-pay practice. Patients are responsible for fees at the time of service; we provide superbills for out-of-network PPO reimbursement. We do not participate in Medi-Cal or HMO panels. If cost is a barrier for your patient, please reach out and we'll discuss options.
