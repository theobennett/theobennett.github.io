Your Journey Starts Here

Taking the first step toward better mental health is a brave and important decision. We are here to make the process as clear and comfortable as possible. This page provides information about what to expect when you become a patient at Altos Psychiatry.

What to Expect on Your First Visit

Your initial appointment is a comprehensive 60–90 minute evaluation. This is a conversation, not an interrogation. We’ll discuss your current challenges, personal and family history, and treatment goals. It’s a collaborative session designed to create a personalized plan that feels right for you.
