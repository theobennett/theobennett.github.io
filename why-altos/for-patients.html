The First Appointment

What to expect

Your initial evaluation is a 60–90 minute conversation — not a questionnaire, not a checklist. We want to understand your full picture: what you're experiencing now, your history, your goals, and what has and hasn't worked before.

By the end of that first session, you'll have our clinical assessment and a proposed treatment plan to discuss. Nothing is decided without your input.

What to bring

  • ✓A list of current medications and dosages
  • ✓Names of prior providers if you'd like records transferred
  • ✓Notes on what's helped and what hasn't — even informally
  • ✓Any questions you'd like to ask us

Intake forms will be sent through our secure patient portal before your appointment. Please complete them at least 24 hours ahead if possible.

Fees & Insurance

Private pay with superbills

We are a private-pay practice, which means you pay at the time of service. We provide detailed superbills — receipts formatted for insurance reimbursement — for patients who wish to seek reimbursement from their PPO insurance carrier.

Out-of-network reimbursement: If you have PPO insurance (common with Bay Area tech employers), you likely have out-of-network benefits that cover 50–80% of the allowable rate after your deductible is met. We recommend calling the member services number on the back of your insurance card and asking about your out-of-network mental health benefits before scheduling.

Care Tiers

Standard and Concierge Plans

We offer two levels of care to match different needs and preferences.

Standard Plan

Core psychiatric care

  • ✓ Medication management within appointments
  • ✓ Telehealth and in-person options
  • ✓ Refill requests within 1 business day
  • ✓ Secure patient portal messaging

Concierge Plan

Elevated access & availability

  • ✓ Everything in Standard
  • ✓ 24/7 nurse triage line
  • ✓ After-hours MD support for urgent needs
  • ✓ Extended scheduling (6 AM – 8 PM)
  • ✓ Patient-preferred communication (text/phone)
  • ✓ Same-day appointments (Premium add-on)

Concierge plan pricing is discussed during your intake call. Plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled with 30 days' notice.
