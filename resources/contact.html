Quick Answers

Before you reach out

How quickly will I hear back?

Within one business day for all inquiries — new patients, referrals, and general questions. If you submit a consultation request over the weekend, expect to hear from us Monday morning.

Do you accept insurance?

Altos Psychiatry is a private-pay practice. We do not bill insurance directly but provide superbills that many patients use for out-of-network reimbursement. Visit our Insurance & Fees page for details and a cost comparison.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes. We offer secure video appointments for follow-up visits throughout California. Initial consultations and certain services (TMS, labs) require in-person visits at our Menlo Park office.

I'm a therapist — how do I refer a patient?

Call (650) 555-0000 or email referrals@altospsychiatry.com. A brief clinical summary is helpful — diagnosis, current medications, and the reason for referral — but we can discuss details over the phone if that's easier.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes — though availability varies by provider and service. We cap our patient panels intentionally to maintain quality of care. If we can't accommodate you immediately, we'll be transparent about wait times and offer interim recommendations.
