We're building something different. If you want to practice psychiatry the way it should be practiced, we'd like to hear from you.

Why this practice is different

Most psychiatric practices ask providers to see more patients, document faster, and be available less. We're building a model that inverts that — smaller panels, better compensation, genuine clinical support, and systems that handle the administrative burden so you can spend your energy on patients.

We believe clinician wellbeing and patient outcomes are the same problem. A burned-out psychiatrist cannot give the care that our model promises.

Sustainable workload

Panel sizes are deliberately limited. You will have time to think.

AI-assisted documentation

Ambient AI scribing reduces documentation time by 50%+. You close charts, not your evening.

Competitive compensation

We pay fairly and transparently. Details shared at interview.
