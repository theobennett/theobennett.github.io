Careers at Altos

We're building a practice where clinicians can do their best work — with sustainable patient loads, modern tools, and a team that takes quality seriously.

Why Altos

A practice built for clinicians, too

The same intentionality we bring to patient care shapes how we treat our team. Burnout is not a feature — it's a design failure we're building around.

Sustainable workload

We deliberately limit panel sizes to protect provider wellbeing and clinical quality. You'll have the time and space to practice the way you were trained to.

AI-assisted documentation

We use ambient AI scribing to dramatically reduce documentation burden, so your energy stays where it belongs — with patients, not with charts.

Integrative clinical culture

We take nutrition, sleep, metabolic health, and emerging treatments seriously. If you share that interest — and ground it in evidence — you'll fit right in.

Competitive compensation

Our private-pay structure supports fair provider compensation without the margin compression of insurance-dependent practices. Good care starts with providers who are valued.

Culture

Who thrives here

Clinicians who want depth

You chose this field because you care about understanding people — not processing volume. Here, you'll have the caseload and appointment length to actually do that.

Lifelong learners

We track emerging evidence across psychopharmacology, interventional psychiatry, metabolic health, and psychotherapy. If you light up when discussing a new RCT, you're our people.

Team-oriented thinkers

We coordinate care internally — medication, therapy, and lifestyle — as a team. That requires mutual respect, clear communication, and the humility to consult when you're unsure.

People who protect boundaries

Sustainable care requires sustainable providers. We build systems — not heroics — to ensure availability. If you believe good boundaries make better clinicians, you'll feel at home.
