Our Philosophy

Psychiatry that supports you across the full arc of your mental health

Most psychiatric practices offer one appointment every month or two. We offer something different: a continuous relationship with a clinician who knows your full picture — and is reachable in moments of urgency.

Our care integrates evidence-based medication management, psychotherapy, and thoughtfully tailored lifestyle modification. We don't treat diagnoses in isolation; we treat people with busy lives and high standards for their own health.

Your Privacy

We understand that for many of our patients, privacy is not a preference — it's a requirement. Our practice is structured accordingly.

We accept private payment and provide superbills for out-of-network reimbursement. Your employer is not involved in your care.

All records, communications, and scheduling are managed through HIPAA-compliant systems. Your information is never sold or shared.
