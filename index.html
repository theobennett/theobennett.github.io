About the Practice

Where academic precision meets
community and connection.

Altos Psychiatry was founded on a simple belief shaped by years of training at Stanford and leading academic medical centers — and by growing up in a state where mental health infrastructure barely existed. We saw what gaps in care can do. We built this practice to close them.

We deliberately keep a smaller patient panel so every individual gets the time, attention, and thoughtful treatment planning that good psychiatric care demands. Our approach integrates medication management, psychotherapy, lifestyle medicine, and outcome tracking into one coordinated practice — not fragmented across disconnected providers.

Evidence-Based Outcomes

We measure what matters.

Every patient's progress is tracked with validated clinical instruments — not guesswork. We use standardized outcome measures at every visit so both you and your provider can see, in data, whether treatment is working.

  • PHQ-9 for depression severity
  • GAD-7 for generalized anxiety
  • PCL-5 for PTSD symptom tracking
  • Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale
  • Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale
  • Personalized treatment goals with milestone tracking

Our TMS program has demonstrated a 68% response rate and 45% remission rate — consistent with published clinical trial data.
