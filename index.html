Altos Psychiatry was founded on a simple belief: exceptional mental health care requires both scientific rigor and deep empathy. Located in the heart of Menlo Park, we provide comprehensive psychiatric services for adults across the San Francisco Peninsula.

Our approach combines the latest evidence-based treatments — including advanced neuromodulation and novel therapeutics — with the thoughtful, personalized attention that only a boutique practice can offer. Every treatment plan is tailored to your unique neurobiology, history, and goals.

Services

A comprehensive treatment toolkit.

From first-line medication management to advanced neuromodulation, we offer the full continuum of psychiatric care under one roof.

Medication Management

Precise psychopharmacology informed by your symptom profile, medical history, and — when indicated — pharmacogenomic testing. We take the time to get it right.

TMS Therapy

FDA-cleared transcranial magnetic stimulation for depression and OCD. Non-invasive, no anesthesia, performed in-office with typical sessions lasting under 20 minutes.

FDA-Cleared

Ketamine Infusions

IV ketamine therapy for treatment-resistant depression, PTSD, and chronic suicidal ideation. Administered in a safe, monitored clinical environment with psychiatric oversight.

Spravato® (Esketamine)

FDA-approved intranasal esketamine for treatment-resistant depression. Administered under REMS protocol in our certified clinic with required post-dose monitoring.

REMS Certified

Psychotherapy

Individual therapy utilizing CBT, ACT, and supportive psychotherapy frameworks. Often integrated with medication management for a comprehensive approach.

Telehealth

Secure video visits for medication management and therapy from anywhere in California. Same quality of care, more flexibility in your schedule.

Evidence-Based Outcomes

We measure what matters.

Every patient's progress is tracked with validated clinical instruments — not guesswork. We use standardized outcome measures at every visit so both you and your provider can see, in data, whether treatment is working.

  • PHQ-9 for depression severity
  • GAD-7 for generalized anxiety
  • PCL-5 for PTSD symptom tracking
  • Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale
  • Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale
  • Personalized treatment goals with milestone tracking

Our TMS program has demonstrated a 68% response rate and 45% remission rate — consistent with published clinical trial data.
