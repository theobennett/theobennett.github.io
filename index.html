Why Altos

Care the way it
should feel.

Most psychiatry is built around volume. We built Altos around you — fewer patients, longer visits, and a team that stays close from the first conversation on.

I.

Unhurried, by design

We deliberately keep a small patient panel. Your first visit is a full hour; follow-ups are never squeezed into fifteen minutes. Time is how good decisions get made.

II.

Stanford-trained expertise

Our clinicians trained at Stanford and leading academic medical centers — and bring that depth to every diagnosis, every medication decision, every plan.

III.

One team, one plan

Medication, therapy, and lifestyle care coordinated inside one practice — not fragmented across providers who never speak. Your full picture stays in one set of hands.

IV.

There when you need us most

Difficult stretches don't keep office hours. Extended evening availability and additional support between visits mean you're never navigating a hard moment alone.

Our Care

Today's essentials, delivered exceptionally — with advanced treatments arriving as the practice grows.

Medication Management

Available Now

Precise psychopharmacology guided by your history, your goals, and — when helpful — pharmacogenomic testing. Longer visits so we have time to get it right.

Psychotherapy

Available Now
