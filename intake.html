What to Expect

Before your visit:

  • You'll receive a reminder email 24 hours before your appointment
  • All your intake forms have been submitted — no additional paperwork needed
  • Have your insurance card ready (if applicable)
  • Test your camera and microphone before the appointment
  • Please arrive 10 minutes early to our Menlo Park office

During your consultation (60 minutes):

  • Your provider will review your history and current symptoms
  • Discuss diagnosis and treatment options
  • Create an initial treatment plan together
  • Answer any questions you have

Need to reschedule or cancel?

Please call us at (650) 555-0000 at least 24 hours in advance.
