Our Clinical Focus

What We Treat

We provide specialized psychiatric care for a broad range of conditions in adults 18 and older.

Many of our patients have lived with their condition for years before finding care that truly fits. Our approach begins with a thorough evaluation — not a checkbox — and a treatment plan that accounts for your full life, not just your diagnosis.

Depression

Major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder (dysthymia), seasonal depression, and postpartum depression. We manage both new-onset and long-standing cases, including patients with incomplete or unsatisfactory responses to previous treatment.

Treatments: Medication management · Psychotherapy · TMS (2026) · Pharmacogenomics

Treatment-Resistant Depression

For patients who have not responded adequately to two or more antidepressant trials. We conduct a thorough re-evaluation of diagnosis, use pharmacogenomic testing where appropriate, and plan a path toward interventional options including TMS and, in the future, ketamine-assisted therapy.

Treatments: Comprehensive re-evaluation · Pharmacogenomics · TMS (2026) · Augmentation strategies

Anxiety Disorders

Generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), social anxiety disorder, panic disorder, specific phobias, and health anxiety. Many high-functioning adults with anxiety have never been formally treated — they've learned to manage. We help identify whether treatment would meaningfully improve quality of life.

Treatments: Medication · CBT-based therapy · Mindfulness-informed approaches

ADHD

Adult ADHD is frequently underdiagnosed, particularly in high-achieving individuals who have developed compensatory strategies. We conduct thorough diagnostic evaluations and offer both stimulant and non-stimulant medication management, alongside coaching-informed therapy approaches.

Treatments: Diagnostic evaluation · Stimulant / non-stimulant medication · Skills-based therapy

PTSD & Trauma

Post-traumatic stress disorder and complex trauma in adults. We offer trauma-informed pharmacotherapy and coordinate closely with trauma-specialized therapists. For patients seeking an integrated approach, we provide both medication management and therapy within our practice.

Treatments: Trauma-informed pharmacotherapy · Psychotherapy · Care coordination

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar I and II disorders, cyclothymia, and bipolar spectrum presentations. Accurate diagnosis is essential — bipolar disorder is frequently misdiagnosed as unipolar depression, leading to ineffective or counterproductive treatment. We prioritize diagnostic clarity before medication decisions.

Treatments: Mood stabilizers · Atypical antipsychotics · Psychoeducation · Therapy

OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder, including contamination OCD, harm OCD, intrusive thoughts, and scrupulosity. We offer both pharmacotherapy and evidence-based therapy (exposure and response prevention, or ERP). TMS has FDA clearance for OCD and will be available in 2026.

Treatments: SRIs / clomipramine · ERP therapy · TMS (2026)

Substance Use

Co-occurring substance use and psychiatric disorders (dual diagnosis). We manage the psychiatric component and coordinate with addiction specialists as needed. We take a non-judgmental, pragmatic approach that meets patients where they are.

Treatments: Co-occurring disorder management · Coordination with addiction specialists
